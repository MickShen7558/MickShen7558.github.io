Description

This course introduces the state-of-the-art machine learning methods for main tasks in 3D computer vision and computer graphics. The purpose of the course is to bring the students to the frontier of machine learning research in 3D computer vision and computer graphics through lectures, paper reading and presentations, group discussions, assignments, and individual course projects.

Each lecture from the 2nd week will consist of 4 paper presentations delivered by students and active in-class discussions driven by students. The instructor team will facilitate the discussions when needed.
